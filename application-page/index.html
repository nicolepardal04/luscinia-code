Luscinia Enterprises General Job Application

Thank you for your interest in working with Luscinia, This job application is intended for us to be able to understand your skills and get an idea of who you are. Ideally, you should be able to complete this entire application in 30-45 minutes. We encourage you to apply, even if you do not have much experience in the field, we will work with you on a plan that lets you contribute to your best capacity. Due to the nature of funding we receive in order to pay salaries of our employees, we are often limited to selecting Canadian citizens, permanent residents or protected persons. Please keep this in mind before applying.
